Surge offers **yield opportunities** without requiring wrapped BTC exposure:


1. **Lend into Pools**
- Deposit stablecoins into default or custom lending pools.


2. **Earn Sustainable Yield**
- Starting at ~**7%+ APY**, with yield sourced from BTC-backed borrowing activity.


3. **Automated Risk Management**
- Choose automated pool strategies or manually configure risk profiles.


4. **Manage Deposits & Withdrawals**
- User interface for deposits, redemptions, and pool configuration.


5. **Stability Pool Participation**
- Optionally allocate into the **Stability Pool**, absorbing liquidations and earning discounted BTC.


6. **Withdrawal Flexibility**
- Withdrawals may be time-gated to balance liquidity and volatility in the system.
