## Market Breakdown

Bitcoin lending today is segmented into three core categories:

- **CeFi platforms**: Centralized, regulated, and focused primarily on large BTC holders. These platforms rely on custodians and often lack transparency.
- **DeFi models (Multisig, P2P, DLCs)**: Non-custodial and promising, but early-stage with UX and regulatory challenges.
- **Wrapped BTC on other chains**: Operate on Ethereum and other L1s with WBTC or similar, introducing bridge risks and custodial dependencies that Bitcoiners often avoid.

## Emerging Trends

- **CeFi dominance** keeps borrowing costs high.  
- **DeFi market share is growing**, supported by infrastructure and regulatory tailwinds.  
- Galaxy reports confirm the rising preference for DeFi in crypto-collateralized lending.  
- Most platforms cater to HNWIs or institutions, leaving **retail Bitcoiners underserved**.  
- As stablecoin usage accelerates, **new payment and remittance channels** are opening up, driving structural changes in the lending landscape.
