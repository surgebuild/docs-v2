## Market Overview

Stablecoins are disrupting traditional finance and are projected to grow from ~$250B today to over **$3 trillion by 2030**. They offer:

- **Low-cost global transactions**: Fraction of traditional banking costs
- **Real-time finality**: Instant settlement compared to traditional systems
- **Programmable financial logic**: Smart contracts enable complex financial operations

## Key Catalysts

### Regulatory Clarity
- **GENIUS Act (2025)**: Regulatory clarity for innovation-driven adoption
- **Institutional Adoption**: Major financial institutions preparing for stablecoin integration
- **Government Support**: Increasing recognition of stablecoins as legitimate financial instruments

## Surge's Vision

Surge positions itself at the intersection of Bitcoin and stablecoins, enabling:

- **Bitcoin-Backed Lending**: Use Bitcoin as collateral for stablecoin loans
- **Cross-Chain Delivery**: Receive stablecoins on preferred blockchain networks
- **Decentralized Infrastructure**: No centralized control or custody
- **Market-Driven Pricing**: Competitive rates determined by supply and demand

This creates a powerful combination that leverages Bitcoin's security and stability while providing access to the rapidly growing stablecoin economy.
