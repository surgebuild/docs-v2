## What Stablecoins enable
- **Low-cost global transactions:** On-chain rails shrink the gap between transaction and settlement, making cross-border payments more efficient.
- **Instant or real-time finality:** Unlike legacy systems that batch and delay, stablecoins settle with finality in seconds.
- **Programmable finance:** With smart contract compatibility, stablecoins unlock automated flows, conditional payments, and dynamic logic. This is where finance meets software.
