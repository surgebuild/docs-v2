When we talk about Bitcoin-backed lending, we have to ask - which Bitcoiners are we really talking about? Institutions and high-net-worth individuals (HNWIs) are already well served.

### Limited Access
Most lending platforms are built with HNWIs in mind. That means retail users face barriers like minimum loan amounts, strict onboarding requirements, or exclusion altogether. 
This leaves everyday Bitcoiners with very few trusted options.

### Higher Borrowing Costs

It‚Äôs not just that APYs are often in the double digits, it‚Äôs that loan terms, flexibility, and pool diversity are also worse.

### Rehypothecation Risk
Most centralized platforms do not disclose whether or how they reuse your Bitcoin. Even when rates are attractive, the risk is unknowable. 
For Bitcoiners, who are ideologically and financially committed to long-term holding, this is a fundamental contradiction.
